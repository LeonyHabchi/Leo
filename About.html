Meet The Crew of Flight 1549

Captain Chesley “Sully” Sullenberger

Veteran pilot with 40+ years of experience, known for his calm leadership and decisive action during the emergency water landing on the Hudson River.

First Officer Jeffrey Skiles

First Officer on his first Airbus A320 flight; worked closely with Sully in the cockpit during the crisis and played a crucial role in handling the controls.

Sheila Dail

Flight Attendant. Helped manage cabin safety and kept passengers calm throughout the emergency.

Donna Dent

Flight Attendant. Played a key role in organizing the evacuation and assisting passengers on the wing of the plane.

Doreen Welsh

Flight Attendant. Despite suffering an injury, she assisted with evacuation efforts in the flooded rear cabin.
